  • Gradius

    First complete project in Unity.

    This is a fan-rebuild of Gradius, the classic Nintendo side-scroller action/shooter where you control a ship with a variety of potential upgrades.

    This remake includes a complete re-build of the 1st level of Gradius: dubbed the 'Classic' build. This level was created to be as true-to-form to the original 1st level as possible. In addition, we added our own 'Custom' level, which implements a new game-mechanic: colored lasers and swappable shields! Your shield is either blue or red, and will provide you with immunity to red or blue damage based upon the color of your shield.

    Dev Time: 2.5 weeks (Sept-19 - Oct-07, 2015)

    INSTRUCTIONS

    TITLE SCREEN
    SWITCH level: RIGHT-SHIFT
    SELECT level: ENTER
    IN-GAME
    Move UP: W
    Move LEFT: A
    Move DOWN: S
    Move RIGHT: D
    ATTACK: N
    POWER-UP: M

    Feel like cheating? Press 'I' to activate invincibility.

    PLAY

    Download Unity Build (PC)

  • Swarm

    Second complete project in Unity, first original game.

    In swarm, the player controls a tank in an alien world, where hundreds of bug-like enemy units are spawned and swarm the player!

    Swarm is a game stylistically modeled after overhead 3rd person action games. Similar to games like "Gauntlet" and "FORCED", the game spawns hordes of units from a various "spawners". These spawners are synced up to musical components, providing the soundtrack for the game as your shred through the swarm.

    Dev Time: 1.5 weeks (Oct-12 - Oct-21, 2015)

    The enemy units are controlled with a standard BOIDs code. Credit for the code goes to Introduction to Game Design, Prototyping, and Development (Part II, Ch. 26), by Jeremy Gibson-Bond. By giving each different unit type a few different parameters, unique behaviours for each type of enemy unit were attained.

    INSTRUCTIONS

    Move UP: W
    Move LEFT: A
    Move DOWN: S
    Move RIGHT: D
    GUNS: Left-click
    FLAMETHROWER: Right-click
    TARGET NUKE: Space (hold)
    FIRE NUKE: Space (release)

    Now with post-game achievements!

    PLAY

    Play Unity Web-build
    Download Unity Build (PC)
