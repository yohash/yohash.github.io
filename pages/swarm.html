Swarm

  • Second complete project in Unity, first original game.

    In swarm, the player controls a tank in an alien world, where hundreds of bug-like enemy units are spawned and swarm the player!

    Swarm is a game stylistically modeled after overhead 3rd person action shooter 'em ups. Similar to games like "Gauntlet" and "FORCED", the game spawns hordes of units from various "spawners". These spawners are synced up to musical components, providing the soundtrack for the game as your shred through the swarm.

    Dev Time: 1.5 weeks (Oct-12 - Oct-21, 2015)

    The enemy units are controlled with a standard BOIDs code. Credit for the code goes to Introduction to Game Design, Prototyping, and Development (Part II, Ch. 26), by Jeremy Gibson-Bond. By giving each different unit type a few different parameters, unique behaviours for each type of enemy unit were attained.

    An imgur album contains a bit more info, and images.

    ENEMIES
    INCHERS
    The brown cubes (animated to look like inch-worms) have low speed, moderate attraction to the player, and high crowd-centering, making them a slow moving blob that creeps across the field.
    BATS
    The bright yellow-pink bats are given high max speed, high velocity-matching, and low attraction to the player, resulting in a large swooping pack that sweeps around the player.
    SPIDERS
    The spider has no crowd-centering, and a large spacing between others desired. The result is an enemy that spreads out across the field and darts in and out of combat.

    The weapons are balanced to provide a lot of fire-power against the horde, but you'll find yourself hard-pressed to keep up! Use nukes against spawners, or kite enemies into large groups. Blaze a trail through the swarm with your flame thrower! And most importantly, never stop shooting!

    Move RIGHT: D
